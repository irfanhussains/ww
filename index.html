About Us

Woodworks interiors is an interior design firm, concerned and sensitive to the physical context of a project and its cultural milieu, located in the heart of Dubai.

We are the leading experts and a one stop solution for all your interior design needs. From concept to implementation, from paper to the walls, there is no one who does it better. Woodworks core competence lies in building up the most modem and ethnic design for your environment. We handle corporate offices, showrooms, warehouses, private villas, restaurants, health clubs. According to the client's requirements our team is committed to provide solutions for all your building needs. With our in house designs, technical and field capabilities, space pattern can ensure that your project is executed to the high quality standards that you expect from market leader.
